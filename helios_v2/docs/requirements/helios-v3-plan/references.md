- **期刊**：Frontiers in Psychology, 2012 (vol. 1, art. 395)

- **核心论点（摘要）**：conscious presence 来源于大脑对内感受信号的预测编码；emotion 是 interoceptive inference 的产物

#### 论文 4：Rochat 2019 — Self-unity as ground zero of learning and development
